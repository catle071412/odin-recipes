Creamy Spinach Dip

Home

Description

I made this recipe for a class once, and it was easily the most loved dish! It's delicious with chips, and you can't resit eating another bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen spinach - thawed, drained, chopped
  • 1/2 cup milke
  • 6 ounces cream cheese (we substituted with sour cream)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 C). Grease a 1 1/2-quart shallow baking dish with 1 tsp olive oil
  2. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in large pot over medium heat. Cook & stir onion & garlic in hot oil until lightly browned, around 4 -7 minutes
  3. Add spinach and simmer until spinach is hot, about 5 minutes. Drain any liquid and transfer to a bowl
  4. Pour milk into pot and return to medium heat. Add cream cheese and melt; cook & stir together, about 3 minutes. Re-add spinach mixture to pot along with 1/2 of mozzarella cheese & all of Cheddar cheese; stir to combine. Season with Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish and top with remaining cheese. Add more to desire
  6. Bake in oven until bubbly and golden brown on top, 20-25 minutes. Option to broil for 3 minutes at the end
