Corn Salad

Home

Description

This easy corn salad recipe is a delicious healthy meal that has a lot of fiber and protein. It can be make with simple but nutritious ingredrients that will keep you full!

Ingredients

For latte:

  • 2 Shots of espresso (or instant coffee)
  • 1 tbsp of cookie butter
  • 1/2 cup
  • Ice

For cold foam:

  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream, cold
  • 1 tsp cookie butter

Steps

For iced latte:

  1. In your glass mix together the hot espresso shots with cookie butter. Stir to combine and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Fill you glass with ice, pour in your cookie butter espresso, and top with cold foam.

For cold foam:

  1. Add to a cup and froth until the mixture is thickened but not as thick as whipped cream.
  2. If you do not have a frother you can whip by hand with a whisk.
